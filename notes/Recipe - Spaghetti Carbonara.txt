Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta
- 2 large eggs
- 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
- 50g Parmesan cheese
- Freshly ground black pepper
- Fresh parsley (for garnish)
Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
2. In a separate pan, cook pancetta until crispy.
3. Whisk eggs and cheese together in a bowl.
4. Drain cooked spaghetti and mix with egg and cheese mixture.
5. Add pancetta and black pepper. Garnish with parsley.
6. Serve immediately.
